Customer Churn Analysis — SaaS Subscription Insights

Analyzed 7,043 customers to identify churn drivers impacting $139K in recurring revenue. Performed feature engineering in Google Sheets, visualized via interactive Looker Studio dashboard.

Key Insights
  • Month-to-month contracts drive the highest churn rate (42%)
  • New customers are highest risk — churn drops sharply after year one
